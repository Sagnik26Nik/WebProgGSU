Haunted Mansion Adventure Game

Team Member: Sagnik Chakrabarti

Project Title: The Haunted Mansion of Beverly Hills

Project Description: Immerse yourself in a fusion of ancient mystery and futuristic dread. The haunted mansion of Beverly Hills holds secrets from a forgotten era, wrapped in a modern, interactive journey. Explore shadowy corridors, solve cryptic puzzles, and navigate through an eerie experience where the past and future collide. Will you uncover the truth, or will the house claim you as its next victim?
